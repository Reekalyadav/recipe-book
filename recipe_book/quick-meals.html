Poha

Ingredients

  • 1½ cups Flattened Rice (Poha)
  • 1 Onion (chopped)
  • 1 Green Chili (chopped)
  • 1 tsp Mustard Seeds
  • 8-10 Curry Leaves
  • ¼ tsp Turmeric Powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tbsp Lemon Juice
  • 2 tbsp Oil
  • 2 tbsp Roasted Peanuts
  • Fresh Coriander for garnish

Instructions

  1. Rinse poha in water and drain well. Keep aside for 10 minutes.
  2. Heat oil in a pan, add mustard seeds and let them splutter.
  3. Add curry leaves, green chili, and chopped onion. Sauté until translucent.
  4. Add turmeric and salt, mix well. Add poha and roasted peanuts.
  5. Mix gently and cook for 2-3 minutes. Turn off heat and add lemon juice.
  6. Garnish with coriander and serve hot.

Upma

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Semolina (Rava/Sooji)
  • 1 Onion (chopped)
  • 1 Green Chili (chopped)
  • 1 tsp Mustard Seeds
  • 1 tsp Urad Dal
  • 8-10 Curry Leaves
  • 2 cups Water
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp Oil or Ghee
  • Fresh Coriander and Lemon Juice for garnish

Instructions

  1. Dry roast semolina in a pan until slightly golden. Set aside.
  2. Heat oil in a pan, add mustard seeds, urad dal, and let them crackle.
  3. Add curry leaves, green chili, and chopped onions. Sauté till soft.
  4. Pour 2 cups water, add salt, and bring to a boil.
  5. Slowly add semolina while stirring continuously to avoid lumps.
  6. Cook until mixture thickens. Turn off heat and garnish with coriander and lemon juice.

Vegetable Noodles

Ingredients

  • 1 pack Noodles (boiled and drained)
  • 1 Onion (sliced)
  • 1 Carrot (julienned)
  • ½ Capsicum (sliced)
  • 1 tsp Soy Sauce
  • 1 tsp Chili Sauce
  • 1 tbsp Oil
  • Salt and Pepper to taste
  • Spring Onion for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. Boil noodles according to packet instructions. Drain and toss with a little oil.
  2. Heat oil in a wok or pan. Add onions, carrots, and capsicum. Stir-fry on high flame for 2-3 minutes.
  3. Add soy sauce, chili sauce, salt, and pepper. Mix well.
  4. Add cooked noodles and toss everything together for 2 minutes.
  5. Garnish with spring onions and serve hot.
